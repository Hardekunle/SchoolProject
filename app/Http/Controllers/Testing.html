Location and Division Preferences

You may apply to programs in up to three different location and division combinations by clicking the Add button below. The division selections below are determined by the location you select. If you do not see the divisions that are of interest to you, please consider choosing a different location. Depending on the division(s) you select, you may also have the option to select a sub division to further indicate you area of preference. In order for your application to be reviewed in a timely manner, you will not be able to amend your location or division preferences once your application is submitted. If invited back for an interview, you will have the opportunity to express interest in other locations or areas of the firm.

Work Authorization - For United States

Do any of the following apply to you?

  • US Citizen or National
  • US Lawful Permanent Resident (i.e., Green Card holder)
  • Person admitted as a Refugee to the US
  • Person granted Asylee status in the US

Please note the above 4 categories do not include the following:

1) Applicants for adjustment of status or asylum, 2) beneficiary of any deferred enforcement action (including deferred action for childhood arrival or "DACA"), 3) beneficiaries of temporary protected status or "TPS", or 4) persons granted temporary work authorization on any basis other than through asylum or refugee status. If your current status is AOS, DACA, TPS or any other temporary work authorization, please answer No.

Are you legally authorized to work in the US? In other words, do you currently hold valid US work authorization (for example, H-1B status, TN visa) or are eligible to apply for  F-1 Optional Practical Training?

Indicate the number of months you have remaining on your current work authorization status (e.g., Optional Practical Training)

If you have not yet used your post-graduate Optional Practical Training, select 12 months

*If eligible, please include your STEM extension; Goldman Sachs is an E-Verify Employer.
